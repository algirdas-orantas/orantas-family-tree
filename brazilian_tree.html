❌❌ ONE-FAMILY HYPOTHESIS DISPROVED (April 2026)
There are TWO separate Jonas Orantas patriarchs in the Kybartai area, not one:

Jonas #1 (jonas_kyb_c1861): b. ~1861, d. 20 Mar 1926, Kybartai town. Wife: Marijona Murauskaitė. Children: Antanas (1901), Anelė (1904).
Jonas #2 (joao_br): Bijotai village, ALIVE 1939. Wife: Marcelė Butkiūtė. Children: Vicente (1908), Justinas (1912), Jonas Jr. (1915).

Proof: Justinas's 1939 marriage record (LVIA/1723/1/10, L.13, Nr.3) states parents "Jonas ir Marcelė Butkiūtė Orantai, iš Bijotų k., gyvi" — but Jonas #1 died in 1926. They may be related (brothers? father/son?) but are NOT the same person.
